OppenEigen

The OppenEigen Team from the Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB) comprises a dynamic group of students specializing in Informatics. Beyond their academic pursuits, this team thrives on a strong sense of camaraderie. They support each other through the highs and lows, fostering a collaborative spirit that fuels their collective success. Individually, they are driven, innovative thinkers, but together, they are a powerhouse of creativity and technological prowess, poised to leave an indelible mark in the world of Informatics.
